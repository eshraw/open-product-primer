# Decision: BET-001 Add product discovery structure to bets

## Status
- Decision: Build now
- Date: 2026-05-27
- Owner: Eshane
- Review date: 2026-06-27

## Why now
- Bets made without surfaced discovery context tend to have weaker rationale and harder kill criteria
- Improving bet quality up front reduces rework and zombie bets downstream

## Alternatives considered
- Link to external docs (Notion/Drive) — discovery stays disconnected from the decision artifact
- Add a discovery section inline to bet-decision.md — conflates decision record with raw research
- New discovery.md file per bet — collocates discovery with the bet, keeps decision record clean

## Expected outcomes
- 100% of new bets include a structured discovery section within 4 weeks (baseline: 0%)

## Kill criteria / rollback trigger
- Teams report the structure adds friction without improving decision quality — remove the feature
